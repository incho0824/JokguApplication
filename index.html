Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Jokgu Club mobile application (“the App”) collects, uses, and shares information.

What we collect

The App is intended for approved club members. During registration and use we may collect:

  • Account information (e.g., name, phone number, birthday)
  • Club participation data (attendance, game sign‑ups) and dues status
  • Basic diagnostics or crash logs to improve the App

We do not collect precise location, contact lists, or advertising identifiers. We do not run third‑party ads.

How we use information

  • To register and authenticate members (including a one‑time keycode for approved members)
  • To manage attendance, game participation, and dues
  • To provide support and improve the App

Third‑party services

The App may open external services such as Venmo for payments and YouTube for related content. When you follow these links, their own privacy policies govern.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share information only as needed to operate the App, comply with legal obligations, or protect members.

Data retention

We retain member data while your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter for club operations. Upon account closure or a verified request, we will delete or anonymize data within a reasonable time.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children

The App is intended for club members. If you believe we have collected information from a minor without appropriate consent, contact us and we will remove it.

Your rights

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data
  • Close your account by contacting us
